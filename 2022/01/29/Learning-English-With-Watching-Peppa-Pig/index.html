1th episode (Muddy Puddles)

abstract

​ After rainning,Peppa and George ran to the garden where there were several muddy puddles.They loved jumping in muddy puddles,but before jumping in muddly puddles,they must wear their boots at first.George jumped in muddy puddles without wearing his boots,fortunately,he had a good sister who reminded him to wear them.They were having a lot of fun with jumping in muddy puddles and the boots protected them from mud.

​ After playing for a while,Peppa found a really big puddle,and George wanted to jump into the puddle first but Peppa stopped him.As elder sister,Peppa should check the puddle was safe or not,so she jumped it first.However,some mud splashed on George when Peppa jumped into the puddle.They didn’t mind mud,and played gladly.They play with mud all over them.After cleaning,the Daddy Pig and Mummy Pig joined them to jump in puddles.

words

  • muddy .adv 泥泞的 .v使变得泥泞，使浑浊
  • puddle .n (水)坑

grammar

  • Run along you two (祈使句说法)

  • Guess what we’ve been donging (现在完成进行时，表示从过去一直持续到现在，并且现在并未停止，还将持续到未来)
